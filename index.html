SmartHomeQR is a simple, secure app for storing and organizing all your smart home device QR codes and setup codes in one place. Whether you're setting up a new home or managing dozens of devices, SmartHomeQR keeps everything organized and instantly accessible.

Smart QR Code Parsing

  • Automatically extracts setup codes, serial numbers, and pairing info
  • Detects device protocol (HomeKit, Matter, Z-Wave, Zigbee, Thread)
  • Identifies manufacturer and model from QR codes
  • Reads text from device labels using advanced OCR
  • Works with Schlage, Ring, Google Nest, Philips Hue, and more

Organize by Home & Room

  • Create multiple homes (house, apartment, office, vacation home)
  • Organize devices by room (living room, kitchen, bedroom, etc.)
  • Quick visual overview of all your devices

Capture & Store QR Codes

  • Photograph QR codes directly from device packaging
  • Import existing photos from your library
  • Store manual setup codes (8-digit pairing codes)
  • High-quality image storage for reliable scanning

Find Devices Instantly

  • Search across all homes and rooms
  • Filter by device category (lights, locks, thermostats, etc.)
  • View fullscreen QR codes ready to scan

Built for Reliability

  • iCloud sync keeps your codes safe and synced across all devices
  • Works offline - your codes are always accessible
  • No account required - just install and start organizing
  • Privacy-focused: your data stays on your devices and iCloud

Perfect For

  • HomeKit and Matter device setup
  • Smart home enthusiasts with many devices
  • Property managers handling multiple locations
  • Anyone tired of searching for device packaging
